  • Introduction

    The SmogDex Teambuilder is an online Pokémon teambuilder that uses analyses from the Smogon Strategy Pokédex. You can pick movesets from the analyses to use in this teambuilder.

    How to Use

    This teambuilder gives you the option of using movesets straight from the Smogon Strategy Pokédex and allows you to modify the movesets from there. Here's out to start building:

    • Pick a Pokémon that you want, in any order.
    • You can change the tier from which the analysis will be taken from. For example, if you want to get a moveset from Azelf's BW OU analysis, you change the tier to Overused. The default tab chosen would be the tier the Pokémon is currently in.
    • Pick the respective moveset and the teambuilder will automatically fill in all the options for you; the EVs, IVs, Ability, Item, Nature, Level, and Moves will be filled.
    • The Ability, Item, Nature, and Moves options all put the respective recommended options at the top so you can differentiate between those in the moveset and the other remaining options.
    • Repeat till you're done; you can opt to just fill in a few Pokémon as well and leave the remaining ones blank.
    • Click the "Exporter" tab at the top and click the "Export!" button. It will export all the Pokémon you entered if they have an ability and at least a move chosen.

    Features

    Besides using movesets from the Smogon Strategy Pokédex, the SmogDex Teambuilder has a few other features as well. The stats of the Pokémon are highlighted with green and red depending on the Pokémon's nature. The stat which is boosted by the nature is highlighted as green; conversely, the stat hindered by the nature is highlighted as red. All individual EVs for each stat is capped at 252. A Pokémon may only use up to 508 EVs. If you exceed the limit, the number below the EVs column will turn red to indicate that. It doesn't prevent you from exporting a Pokémon with more than 508 EVs though. The "Go to Pokédex!" button will lead you to the analysis of the Pokémon chosen. Don't worry about losing your changes on the teambuilder! It opens the page in a new tab. It will also bring you directly the the moveset if you have chosen one. If a Pokémon is not chosen, it will lead you to the main page of the Strategy Pokédex. You can also reset a Pokémon by changing the Pokémon option to blank, which is the first option. Most of the features are already implemented, but stay tuned for more updates!
